About Brent & Co.

Technology for culture, community, and opportunity.

Brent & Co. builds technology that helps people discover culture, build community, and create opportunity.

Founder-led by Shalanda Brent, the company is the home for a growing collection of digital products designed to feel useful, human, and close to real life.

What Brent & Co. Builds

Brent & Co. creates practical digital products for people cooking, creating, rebuilding, learning, and connecting. The work is polished and intentional, but the heart is simple: technology should help people move through real life with more confidence and support.
