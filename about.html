About Brent & Co

A warm technology studio building tools for real life.

Brent & Co is a growing family of digital platforms for music, culture, careers, cooking, and community. Each app has its own purpose, but the mission is shared: make useful technology feel human, approachable, and connected to everyday needs.

The ecosystem includes Find The Beat, Let’s Cook Y’all, Second Chance Careers, and BEU. Together, they support creative discovery, career rebuilding, food education, and cultural exploration.
