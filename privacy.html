Privacy Policy

Brent & Co builds technology that helps people discover culture, build community, and create opportunity. This policy explains how we handle information across Brent & Co and connected products.

Last Updated: July 10, 2026

Products Covered

This Privacy Policy applies to Brent & Co. and products that may use Brent & Co. accounts, shared authentication, or related services, including Let’s Cook Y’all, Find The Beat, and Second Chance Careers.

Some connected products may provide additional notices when a feature needs product-specific information.

Information Users Provide

We may collect information you choose to provide, such as your name, email address, profile photo, account details, profile information, support messages, preferences, saved content, and user-generated content such as profiles, posts, recipes, uploads, notes, messages, career information, or other materials you submit.

Google Sign-In and OAuth Data

If you sign in with Google, we may receive information made available through Google OAuth, such as your name, email address, Google account identifier, and profile image. We use this information for account authentication, sign-in, account linking, and access to connected Brent & Co. products.

Device, Browser, and Usage Information

We may collect device and browser details, IP address, pages viewed, referring pages, general location derived from technical information, cookie or session identifiers, analytics events, error logs, and performance information to keep the services working and improve the experience.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies for sign-in sessions, security, preferences, analytics, and basic site functionality. Your browser may allow you to limit cookies, but some account features may not work correctly without them.

How Information Is Used

  • Provide, operate, and maintain Brent & Co. products and account services.
  • Authenticate users and support Google Sign-In, Brent accounts, and connected product access.
  • Personalize profiles, saved content, preferences, and user experience.
  • Respond to communications, privacy requests, support needs, and legal notices.
  • Monitor performance, troubleshoot errors, protect accounts, and improve reliability.
  • Send service messages, account notices, and push notifications where users have enabled them.
  • Develop new features and understand how people use the services.

How Information May Be Shared

Brent & Co. does not sell users’ personal information.

We may share information with service providers and hosting platforms that help operate the services, including infrastructure, authentication, analytics, storage, email, security, and support tools. We may also share information when needed to comply with law, enforce terms, protect rights and safety, respond to lawful requests, or complete a business transition such as a merger or transfer of assets.

When you use connected products, account information may be shared within the Brent & Co. ecosystem to support sign-in, profile access, memberships, and product-specific functionality.

User Content and Public Areas

Some product features may allow public profiles, creator pages, posts, uploads, recipes, comments, career materials, or other content. Information you choose to make public may be visible to others.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information. No internet service can guarantee absolute security, and users should keep account credentials private.

Data Retention

We keep information as long as needed to provide services, maintain accounts, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and support legitimate business needs. Retention periods may vary by feature and product.

Account and Data Deletion Requests

You may request account deletion or data deletion by contacting us. We may need to verify your identity and may retain limited information where required for security, legal, fraud prevention, or recordkeeping purposes.

User Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, portability, restriction, or objection related to your personal information. You may also have the right to withdraw certain consents. To make a privacy request, contact us using the information below.

Children’s Privacy

Brent & Co. products are not intended to collect personal information from children under 13 without appropriate consent. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us so we can review and respond.

Third-Party Links and Services

Our products may link to third-party websites or services. Their privacy practices are controlled by those third parties, not Brent & Co. Review their policies before sharing information with them.

International Users

If you access the services from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States or other locations where service providers operate.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The Last Updated date shows when the policy was most recently revised. Continued use of the services means the updated policy applies.

Contact Information

For privacy requests or questions, contact Brent & Co. at shalanda.brent@gmail.com.
