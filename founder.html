Founder statement

Built from experience. Designed for real people.

I built Brent & Co for people who are creating their next chapter. Some are looking for work. Some are looking for creative partners. Some are learning how to cook, travel, rebuild, or be seen. I wanted the technology to feel useful, warm, and grounded in real life.

Founder mission

Build with care. Launch with purpose. Keep people at the center.

Brent & Co exists to turn lived experience into tools people can actually use.

Mission

Create platforms that make opportunity, creativity, learning, and community easier to reach.

The work is personal, but the goal is practical: help people move forward with clearer tools and stronger support.

Vision

Founder bio

A venture studio with a human reason.

A U.S. Army veteran, software developer, UX designer, and entrepreneur, Shalanda created Brent & Co as a home for products that solve real problems with care. The studio’s platforms span music networking, culinary education, workforce support, and cultural discovery, all built around the belief that good technology should help people move with more confidence.

Company timeline

The Brent & Co path.

2022

Find The Beat concept

The first architecture for a music networking platform took shape around search, profiles, uploads, showcases, and messaging.

2024

Brent & Co ecosystem

The studio vision expanded into a connected family of platforms for music, food, careers, culture, and community.

2025

Launch-ready foundation

Backend accounts, databases, profile systems, dashboards, and production deployments became the priority.

2026

Founder control center

Brent & Co moved toward shared identity, founder analytics, admin tools, and early user growth across the apps.
