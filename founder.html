Founder statement

Built from experience. Designed for real people.

I built Brent & Co because I know what it feels like to start over, rebuild, and create something from the ground up. These platforms were created for real people with real needs - people looking for work, people looking for creative connection, and people looking for community. I did not wait for permission. I built it. Now I am focused on making sure it reaches the people it was built for.

Founder bio

A venture studio with a human reason.

Shalanda Brent is the founder and CEO of Brent & Co, a venture studio focused on building practical technology that improves lives. A veteran, software developer, UX designer, and entrepreneur, she created Find The Beat, Let’s Cook Y’all, and Second Chance Careers to help people connect, create, learn, and grow. Her work combines technology, community, and real-world problem solving to create platforms that serve everyday people.

Company timeline

The Brent & Co path.

2022

Find The Beat concept

The first architecture for a music networking platform took shape around search, profiles, uploads, showcases, and messaging.

2024

Brent & Co ecosystem

The studio vision expanded into a connected family of platforms for music, food, careers, culture, and community.

2025

Launch-ready foundation

Backend accounts, databases, profile systems, dashboards, and production deployments became the priority.

2026

Founder control center

Brent & Co moved toward shared identity, founder analytics, admin tools, and early user growth across the apps.
